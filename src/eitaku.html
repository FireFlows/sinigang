Eitaku is the ship name between Eito Aotsuki and Takumi Sumino from the 2025 video game The Hundred Line -Last Defense Academy-. Depending on who you think tops or bottoms, the ship is sometimes called takuei (with Takumi topping/Eito bottoming). I don't really have a preference, but I'll be using eitaku here since it's the most common name among English-speaking fans.

It's truly the perfect ship for me. I love ships fueled by mutual hatred that turn into homoeroticism (or were homoerotic to begin with!). I love obsessive ships.
