About webrings
Webrings are a list of a bunch of websites. 
The websites are looped from beginning to end, creating
a ring shape Hence, webring.
Webrings often follow a theme: there can be a webring
of video game fans, or music fans, or fans of specific
kinds of music, or people who live in a certain 
country, really anything that you can categorize people/sites by.

About The Ring of Fire
The Ring of Fire is a tectonic belt (not ring, fun misnomer) of earthquakes and volcanoes. 
These occur on the Pacific Ocean, so it's also called the Pacific Ring of Fire.
This has nothing to do with my webring. The webring "The Ring of Fire" is for me, since
my name is Fire! It's my webring!
